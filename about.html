History

Qtrac Ltd. was founded in 2006, traded until 2024, and was wound up in 2025.*

The “Qtrac” in Qtrac Ltd. stood for “Quality Training Research And Consultancy”. Qtrac Ltd. began with the intention of providing programming training courses. However, in its first eight years it mostly earned money from the books written by Mark Summerfield its owner and sole employee, plus income from providing technical writing services and programming training courses. During Qtrac Ltd.'s last decade, most of its income was generated by license sales of its PDF comparison products, in particular the DiffPDF GUI application and the more technical comparepdfcmd tool (itself the successor to the diffpdfc tool).

For many years we also produced a wide variety of free open source software, mostly small libraries and utilities created using many different programming languages, as well as a free open source DiffPDF application now maintained by others. However, In view of the EU’s Cyber Resilience Act and an abundance of caution, we have withdrawn all our free software.

* Qtrac Ltd was a Digital River/MyCommerce customer and its closure was precipitated by Digital River's refusal to pay its debts to its customers.
